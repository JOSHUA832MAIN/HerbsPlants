Agrimony

Agrimony (Agrimonia eupatoria) is a perennial herb known for its medicinal properties and distinctive appearance. It belongs to the rose family (Rosaceae) and is native to Europe, but it can also be found in North America and parts of Asia.

Chia Plant

Chia (Salvia hispanica) is a flowering plant belonging to the mint family (Lamiaceae). It is native to Central America and was cultivated by the Aztecs and Mayans for its seeds, which are now popular as a superfood due to their high nutritional value.

Mint

Mint (Mentha) is a fragrant herb that belongs to the Lamiaceae family. It is widely used in cooking, traditional medicine, and various personal care products.

Aloe Vera

Aloe Vera (Aloe barbadensis miller) is a succulent plant known for its thick, fleshy leaves and numerous health benefits.

Basil

Basil (Ocimum basilicum) is a fragrant herb known for its culinary uses and traditional medicine.
